Model: Rumor Propagation

Cell Space
2D grid representing individuals in a social network.

Cell States
0 = Unaware
1 = Spreading
2 = Inactive

Neighborhood
Moore neighborhood (8 surrounding cells)

Initial Condition
One individual initially spreading the rumor.

Basic Rules
1. If an unaware individual has at least one spreading neighbor, they become spreading.
2. A spreading individual becomes inactive after some time.
3. Inactive individuals remain inactive and do not spread the rumor.
